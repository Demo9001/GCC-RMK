Young Employees Network

A socially and professionally supportive group helping young employees across the organisation to connect with each other and access training, mentoring, support and networking opportunities.

Disability Confident Employer

Enabling the council to create an inclusive and diverse working environment and county, helping with sign-posting and peer-support, and providing 'lived experience' on Black, Asian and Minority Ethnic (BAME) issues.

Disability Network

A group of like-minded employees working together to productively challenge issues that may prevent staff with a disability, and ensuring that all members of staff can lead full and rewarding careers with the council.



PRISM

Prism is the LGBT+ staff network, creating safe, inclusive and diverse working environments that encourage a culture of respect and equality for everyone, regardless of their sexual orientation and/or gender identity.

Stonewall Acceptance

A small group of people working for Gloucestershire County Council, Gloucester City Council, or Gloucestershire Fire and Rescue (GFRS), trying to juggle working life with caring for someone who is unable to look after themselves.
